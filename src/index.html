Functionality overview

This app provides Google authentication.

You can choose one of three canvas size (32×32px, 64×64px, 128×128px) and use 3 tools: pen (has 4 size), eraser, paint bucket. Also there is a frame management and preview areas.

You can download the drawn animation in "apng" format.
